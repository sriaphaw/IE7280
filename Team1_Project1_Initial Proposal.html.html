Initial Proposal      IE7280       Spring2021

Mei-Chun Hung   Soumyajeet Patra   Wasinee Opal Sriapha

Introduction

Thank u, nextYou have heard that everyone nowadays uses dating applications (a.k.a. apps) and many people even found the love of their life online. Indeed, previous research has confirmed that dating apps have been in the mainstream and become part of our culture for the past decade. In the age of COVID-19 in combination with the cultural acceptance of online dating, the number of dating apps as well as their diversity have increased immensely over the past few years. The byprodcut of the phenomenon is an enormous amount of data, a treasure for data enthusiasts.

Objective

Given the vast variety of dating apps with different popularity levels available across Google Play, a digital distribution platform, this study aims to understand a number of dependent variables (e.g., rating scores of dating apps, service prices, etc.) by analyzing several independent variables to unveil the level of impact each independent variable may have on any of the dependent ones. In addition to finding any potential correlation between two variables, the study intends to search for trends in data while analyzing the popularity, pricing strategy, and customer satisfaction data of the dating apps.

Dataset

  File Description
Source Google Play Store
Description of Data File The dataset consists of 67 dating apps with 1,000,000 as the minimum number of downloads on Google Play as of January 29, 2021.
Extraction Method Web scraping into .csv file
File Type .csv
Extraction Date 29-Jan-21

  Attribute Definition
Name Public name of dating app
Description Advertised description of dating app on Google Play
Category Google Play’s category chosen by the developer
Installs Estimate number of installs
Score App Store Rating of the app
No_of_Rating Number of customers rated the app
Min_Product_Price Minimum price of paid service
Max_Product_Price Maximum price of paid service
Size Mobile app file size in MB
Ads Whether the app displays Ads
